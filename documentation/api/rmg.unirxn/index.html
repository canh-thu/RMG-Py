4. The rmg.unirxn Package¶

The rmg.unirxn package contains classes and functions for working with unimolecular reaction networks. These networks are unusual in that there are pressure-dependent phenomenological rate coefficients for each isomer or product channel – local minima on the potential energy surface – to every other isomer and product channel in the network, not just those directly adjacent.
